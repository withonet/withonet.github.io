ELLIOT ALLEN | DIRECTOR

ABOUT | WORK | CONTACT

ELLIOT ALLEN (they/them/he/him) is a queer & trans director, designer, playwight, and performer who works with emerging playwrights to bring new works to life. An alum of NYU Tisch's Playwrights Horizons Theater School, they've directed, written, and designed several PHTS productions. Most recently, Elliot's work has been seen in productions from the Invitation Arts Collective (puppet design, For Water by Zofia Alberti) and Foul Fiend Theatre Troupe (lighting coordination, The Body by Zoe Gray). He is also in the process of writing animal songs, a collection of songs exploring how the nonhuman world can inform human relationships.
